Personal projects

I'm a big fan of the open-source methodology and all types of volunteer-driven projects.

  • Front-end Development South Africa (FEDSA)

    A 3000+ member, non-profit organisation that I co-founded with two good friends: Justin Slack and Shailen Naidoo . The goal of FEDSA is to support and advocate for the advancement of front-end development in South Africa.

  • ZA Tech and ZA Product Design

    I spend a fair bit of time attending to administrative and moderation tasks on both ZA Tech, a 10 000+ member Slack workspace used by the South Africa tech community at large, and it's sister workspace ZA Product Design.

  • Codebridge

    I used to formally oversee this 1500+ member organisation dedicated to the exploration of the intersection between technology and social good. However, I recently stepped down in my official capacity, but still remain involved behind the scenes.
